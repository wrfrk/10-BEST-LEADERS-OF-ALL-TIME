Abraham Lincoln

Abraham Lincoln, born in 1809 in a log cabin in Kentucky, is remembered as one of the greatest leaders in history. Despite a difficult childhood and limited formal education, Lincoln became a self-taught lawyer and politician. Elected president in 1860, his leadership was defined by the American Civil War and his commitment to preserving the Union.
